Why We Created WorkOut Logs

You work hard every day. You do everything humanly possible to meet your family's needs and make sure they are healthy and happy. One day, you are on a family vacation and go to the nearest gym but you realize you forgot your workout journal at home. What do you do? Well, we have been in your shoes before, and decided to take the frustration off your shoulders and create a WorkOut Log app so no matter where you are you can always keep track of your daily exercise routines.


Welcome to WorkOut Log! Throw away your paper exercise journal and use our app where your workout info is securely stored and easily retrieved on any device. You can effortlessly create an account, enter and organize your exercises, log your body weight, view or edit past data, as well as backup and store your exercise journal into your secure cloud account.
